Why Do We Exist?

The key players in a school ecosystem are the Children, Teachers, Management and other staffs; affiliated to a state or central board of studies. Initiating and running an educational institution is one of the most divine yet challenging jobs undoubtedly as they form an instrumental part in designing and empowering a generation to drive humanity forward. They are envisioned with the goal to provide holistic and constructive learning experience to their kids, but often because of other organizational and system requisties the focus tend to shift slightly into making this a place where we ensure portions are completed and exams are happening at their given frame of time.

The natural learning pace, chance to inspire natural and acquired intelligences often do not go beyond a specific spectra. The Multiple Intelligence Theory, by Dr. Howard Gardener accounts to the fact that human beings have a number of relatively discrete intellectual capacities, to name : Linguistic, Visual-Spatial, Musical, Bodily-Kinesthetic, Logical-Mathematical, Naturalistic, Existential, Interpersonal and Intrapersonal.

As the current system we know, focus a little too much on logical-mathematical and linguistic intelligence, the need to introduce and give kids a chance to explore other intelligence boosting activities, we feel will make a life-changing impact in their decision making and individual capabilities. The theory of multiple intelligences proposes a major transformation in the way our schools are run. It suggests that teachers be trained to present their lessons in a wide variety of ways using music, cooperative learning, art activities, role play, multimedia, field trips, inner reflection etc. which we know, would be a little to much to ask in between the already filled academic calender and portion plans.

Hence, here we are trying to support you and in the pursuit to ensure the creativity and uniqueness of every kid is celebrated and cherished at its best.

What are we doing?

Step 1 : Please fill in the form.
Step 2 : Complete the sign up process with a telephonic conversation with the Revive Team.
Step 3 : Acknowledge upon receiving weekly mails either through the school ID or to respective club-incharge faculty/student.
Step 4 : Allocate 1 hour in the week to carry out the activity as mentioned in the Activity kit, shared along with the mail.
Step 5 : The pictures of the activity or the activity output will in one way or the other a celebration of their creativity, hence please feel to share them through the school's social media handle. (We're here in case you find it difficult to)
Step 6 : Acknowledge the feedback mail by filling a form enclosed along the mail before Monday to receive the next kit.
Step 7 : We're a community of educational enthusiasts who wish to see a positive change in the way education and educational institutions function in our country, If you feel this project is making a impact in helping you achieve organic growth, please contribute to us here.

No issues whatsoever. We're only adamant in terms of the feedback mail, this is to constructively enrich the activity kits.

How are we doing this?

All the activities are designed with the vision to nurture one particular or a set evident multiple intelligence skill sets in an individual. By enacting or carrying out these small activities in groups we wish to provide the kid a journey to identify their natural skill set and increase its true potential. The impact of the activites will be gauged upon their influence and response from the teachers and students.

During School Sign up, we'd like to have a telephonic conversation with the concerned authority in school just once to identify the available resources and design activities custom to the vision and mission of the school.

After signing up we will share a mail every monday(Start of the week) with a kit for each club containing an activity around their theme. The activity will have a set of quantifiable objectives and outputs relevant to the academic curriculum or societal background which can be used to upskill learnings within the school ecosystem.

In short, we see Schools as super amazing spaces and we also see some clubs inactive so the attempt here is to REVIVE these clubs to bring organic growth into schools and most importantly, help kids identify and design their natrual learning pace and most importantly be naturally happy, inorder to be academically performing.

We'de also like to send you over an annual report with the details and pictures(if you share) of the activites carried out in the year. We're very very excited to have your school join the list!
